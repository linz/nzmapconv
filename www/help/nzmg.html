New Zealand Map Grid coordinates

Description

New Zealand Map Grid is a projection coordinate system defining positions in terms of the NZGD1949 datum. More information about this coordinate system can be found at www.linz.govt.nz/geodetic/datums-projections-heights/projections/new-zealand-map-grid. Positions are define in terms of metres east and north of a reference point.

Entering coordinates

Coordinates may be entered as either easting then northing, or northing then easting. Each coordinate can include fractions of a metre, and can be followed by "mE" for eastings and "mN" for northings (though this is not necessary). The coordinates may be separated by a comma, or by spaces.

Examples of valid coordinates are: "2753721,6119120", "6119120 2753721", and "2753721.8 mE,6119120.12 mN"
