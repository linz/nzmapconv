New Zealand Transverse Mercator coordinates

Description

New Zealand Transverse Mercator 2000 is a projection coordinate system defining positions in terms of the NZGD2000 datum. More information about this coordinate system can be found at www.linz.govt.nz/geodetic/datums-projections-heights/projections/new-zealand-map-grid. Positions are define in terms of metres east and north of an origin.

Entering coordinates

Coordinates may be entered as either easting then northing, or northing then easting. Each coordinate can include fractions of a metre, and can be followed by "mE" for eastings and "mN" for northings (though this is not necessary). The coordinates may be separated by a comma, or by spaces.

Examples of valid coordinates are: "1843713,5557428", "5557428 1843713", and "1843713.8 mE,5557428.12 mN"
